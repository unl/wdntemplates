What is RSS ?

RSS, or Really Simple Syndication, is an open-standard XML format used to publish frequently updated works such as blog entries, news headlines, audio, and video. You can subscribe to these feeds by using a news reader program. The application connects to the news services at preset intervals and downloads new items as they are published. Learn More…
